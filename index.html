ScoreTracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: 02/21/2025

Data Collection

The ScoreTracker application collects device identifiers (such as device ID, Android ID) for the purpose of app functionality and performance analysis. These identifiers are not shared with third parties and are not stored in a way that allows identification of individual users.

Device Permissions

The application does not request any special permissions to operate on your device, but it may collect device identifiers necessary for functionality and performance improvements.

Target Audience

ScoreTracker is designed for a general audience and can be used by children aged 6 and up. No personal or sensitive data from children is collected or shared.

Sensitive Content

The application does not contain any sensitive or inappropriate content. It is suitable for family use.

Security

Although we do not collect any personal data, we have implemented security measures to ensure the application is safe and respects the privacy of all its users, including children. The device identifiers collected are stored securely and used solely for the purpose of enabling app functionality and improving app performance.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.
